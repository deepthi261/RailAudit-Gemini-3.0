Based on the `METRIC_DEFINITIONS` and backend logic I implemented in your dashboard code, here is the definition of the **ERA-2024 Protocol**:

**ERA-2024 (Energy Regulation Act 2024)** is the compliance standard used by the RailAudit Agent to verify financial efficiency. Specifically, it enforces the **Fuel Index Regulation**, which mandates:

> **"Fuel costs must not exceed 15% of total Working Expenses."**

### Where it appears in your system:
1.  **Dashboard (Frontend)**: In the "Compliance & Agency" card, the `Status` field checks against this rule. If the fuel spend varies significantly from this 15% cap, the Agent flags it.
2.  **Backend Logic**: The server cites `Parliamentary Bill #405` as the cross-reference for this protocol during the audit process.

This protocol acts as the "Ground Truth" for the AI Agent to decide if the financial report is **Compliant** or needs **Agency Intervention**.
